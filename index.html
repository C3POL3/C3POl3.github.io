A little bit about myself, I was born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico. I am a full-time college student at Central Central New Mexico Community College, attending for an associate's of applied science degree in Computer Information systems, with a concentration in Cybersecurity.

As a child, I've always been interested in computers and systems. There was something about them that I found so facinating, how they worked physically with hardware and how the Operating System of a computer interacted with software and components within that system. Linux was a huge part of my life growing up. One of my first systems was a broken laptop with a missing screen, and I had figured out that you could connect it to a monitor to see viual output. The laptop had an old drive in it, it was an old installation of Windows. I would experiment with the Operating Systems, breaking them, fixing them, then I realized that I could use something called a Virtual Machine. This was a game changer for me.

Instead of installing systems on the hardware itself over and over again, I could do it through a hypervisor and keep my original system intact. I have an obession with virtual machines. The first software I used was Oracle VirtualBox. I then moved on to different software and hypervisor, I currently now use QEMU/KVM. My facination with virutal machines has lead me to experimenting on so many systems, either with malware, webservers, DNS Servers, content controllers, malware analysis, pentesting labs, etc. I have fun with virtual machines.

I also have some experience with HTML, CSS, PHP and JS, athought it is limited, I would sometimes host local webserver on my network just for fun and update them, even though I knew no one else could see them. It was like a little easter egg for me. I found making these webpages fun, I even created some fun interactive ones (they were usually broken) but some functioned. Now, I would like to improve my skills in HTML, CSS and JS, I think it would be a very good skillset to have and help me take my career further. I understand the basic elements of these web technologies but I would like to understand them more in depth.

Now I have a career to look forward too. I want to be a Red Team Operator, Ethical Hacker, Malware Analyst, Digital Forensisist, etc. There are so many goals I have in my life and so many careers I want to pick from.
